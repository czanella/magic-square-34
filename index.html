A magic square is a table of numbers where the numbers in each row, column and diagonal always add up to the same result. This site is a homage to a one-of-a-kind magic square.

German artist Albrecht Dürer's engraving Melencolia I contains a 4x4 magic square where not only the rows, columns and diagonals add up to 34 but also several other interesting patterns in it also add up to 34 - such as the numbers in the four corners and the numbers in the central square. This site explores all such patterns in Dürer's square.
